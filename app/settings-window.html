Welcome

To upload a cropped portion of your screen use the keyboard shortcut:

command + shift + 4 alt + shift + 4


To upload your entire screen use the shortcut:

command + shift + 3 alt + shift + 3


Uploads

Pussh requires an upload service to function. Choose a service in the uploads tab, and configure it in the service tab. If you are unsure which to use, try out Imgur. Imgur is free, requires no setup or hosting, and is the default upload service. Use the keyboard shortcut now to see Pussh in action!
